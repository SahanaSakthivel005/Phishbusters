Advanced Incident Response and Forensics

    Digital Forensics Techniques: Investigating cybercrimes by collecting, preserving, and analyzing digital evidence from computers, networks, and other digital devices.
    Malware Analysis and Reverse Engineering: Studying malicious software to understand its behavior, origins, and impact. Reverse engineering involves dissecting malware to uncover its design and functionality.
    Incident Response Automation: Using automated tools and processes to quickly detect, respond to, and mitigate cyber incidents. This helps in minimizing the impact of attacks and recovering systems efficiently.
