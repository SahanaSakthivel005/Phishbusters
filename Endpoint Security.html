Endpoint Security

    Antivirus and Antimalware: Antivirus and antimalware programs are the vigilant sentries on your devices, constantly scanning for malicious software (malware) and swiftly neutralizing threats to keep your system safe.
    Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR): EDR solutions are like advanced security systems in your digital fortress. They monitor endpoints (like computers and mobile devices), detect suspicious activities, and respond to threats in real-time, ensuring quick containment and remediation.
    Secure Configurations and Hardening: Hardening your endpoints means setting up your devices with strong security measures, like disabling unnecessary services and enforcing strict access controls. It's like turning your castle into an impregnable fortress.
