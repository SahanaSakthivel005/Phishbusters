Compliance and Legal Issues in Cybersecurity

    GDPR, CCPA, and Other Data Protection Regulations: Regulations like GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) that govern how personal data is collected, stored, and protected.
    Cybersecurity Laws and Policies: Legal frameworks and policies that define acceptable behaviors, responsibilities, and penalties related to cybersecurity. These laws aim to protect information systems and data.
    Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing: Authorized testing of computer systems and networks to identify security vulnerabilities. Ethical hackers use the same techniques as malicious hackers but in a legal and controlled manner.
    Privacy and Data Breach Notification Laws: Regulations requiring organizations to protect personal data and notify affected individuals and authorities in the event of a data breach. These laws aim to ensure transparency and accountability.
